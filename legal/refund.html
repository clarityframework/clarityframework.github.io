Refund Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-12

All sales are final

Due to the instant-access nature of this digital product, all sales are final once access has been provided.

Exceptions

We will consider a refund only in the following limited cases:

  • Duplicate charge for the same purchase (accidental double payment).
  • Access failure where access cannot be delivered after reasonable troubleshooting.

Before requesting a refund

  • Do not repurchase.
  • Open your order confirmation email and use “View order”.
  • Click “Open My Reading” again in a normal browser window (not private mode).
  • Disable aggressive privacy extensions temporarily if needed.

How to request

Email support@clarityframework.co with your order email, approximate purchase time, and a short description of the issue.
