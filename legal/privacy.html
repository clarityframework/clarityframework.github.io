Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-12

1) What we collect

  • Purchase information (processed by our payment provider): email, payment status, order metadata.
  • On-page query parameters such as name may be used only to personalize your reading page greeting.
  • Local storage on your device may store non-sensitive keys to enable access flow and recovery (e.g., last opened reading URL).

2) What we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect or store full assessment answers on our site.
  • We do not sell personal data.

3) How we use data

  • To deliver access to your purchased reading.
  • To provide basic support and troubleshoot access issues.
  • To maintain security and prevent abuse.

4) Cookies and local storage

Our reading and bridge pages may use local storage to store temporary access keys and the last opened reading URL. Private browsing and privacy extensions may block this, which can prevent access.

5) Third-party services

Payments and order confirmations are handled by our payment provider. Their processing is subject to their own policies.

6) Data retention

Local storage data remains on your device unless you clear browser data. Order data is retained by the payment provider according to their policies and legal requirements.

7) Your choices

  • You can clear local storage in your browser settings (may prevent access recovery).
  • You can contact us to request support regarding access issues.

8) Contact

Privacy questions: privacy@clarityframework.co
